Welcome to SiPP(Simple Privacy Policy), a place to navigate privacy policies with ease.

While installing/using applications it would be great to skim through the Privacy Policies before accepting them. While many policies are too long to read, others need a degree in law to just understand them.

On conducting a survey, Privacy Policies are
  • Too long
  • Hard to understand
To address this very problems, we introduce SiPP(Simple Privacy Policy), a tool where the large barral is containerized into small cups to sip from :)
